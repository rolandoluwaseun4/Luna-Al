Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Luna AI is built by Roland Oluwaseun Omojesu, a self-taught developer from Nigeria. This policy explains what data Luna collects, why, and how it's protected. We keep it simple because we believe you deserve to actually understand it.

1. What we collect

When you use Luna AI, we collect:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and encrypted password when you sign up. If you use Google sign-in, we receive your name and email from Google.
  • Conversation history — the messages you send and Luna's responses, stored so Luna can remember context across sessions.
  • Profile preferences — your name, birthday (optional), favorite topics, and personality settings you configure in Settings.
  • Usage data — how many messages you've sent today, which model you used, and last activity time. Used to enforce fair-use limits.
  • Push notification subscriptions — your browser's push subscription token if you enable notifications.
  • Uploaded files — images, PDFs, and videos you share with Luna during conversations. These are processed to generate responses and not stored permanently.

2. What we don't collect

  • We do not collect payment information (Luna is free)
  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers
  • We do not read your conversations for advertising purposes
  • Secret Mode conversations are never saved to our database

3. How we use your data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide Luna's AI responses — your messages are sent to AI providers (Groq, OpenRouter, Google) to generate replies
  • Maintain conversation context so Luna remembers what you've discussed
  • Send push notifications if you've opted in
  • Enforce daily usage limits to keep the service fair and available for everyone
  • Improve Luna over time — we may review anonymized conversation patterns (never linked to your identity)

4. Third-party AI providers

Luna uses the following AI services to process your messages:

  • Groq — fast AI inference for most responses
  • OpenRouter — fallback AI models when Groq is unavailable
  • Google Gemini — image understanding and some advanced reasoning
  • Cloudflare Workers AI — image generation

Your messages are sent to these services to generate responses. Each service has their own privacy policy. We do not share your account information or identity with these providers — only the message content needed to generate a response.

5. Data storage

Your data is stored in MongoDB Atlas, a cloud database service. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard security practices including JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, and HTTPS for all connections.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — ask us what we have stored about you
  • Delete your account — request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your conversations — request a copy of your conversation history
  • Opt out of notifications — disable push notifications at any time in Settings
  • Use Secret Mode — have conversations that are never stored

7. Children's privacy

Luna AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Cookies

Luna uses a single authentication cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of Luna after changes means you accept the updated policy.
